Closed environment contamination...

Closed Indoor Environment is polluted due to below 3 types of contaminants summarized in terms of potential hazard to human health
1. Microbial Pathogens: Allergens, Bacteria, Viruses and Fungi, Air born or Surface Grown / Deposited, creates immediate harm
2. Chemicals / Harmful Gas Molecules: Bad Odor Causing Gases such as combination of Methane and Ammonia, SO2, CO2, NO2, H2S, CO.. Mostly generated due to external pollutant sources or generated during operation of indoor machines/equipments or various chemicals used in day to day activities or food preparations.
3. Suspended Particulate Matter: The micro meter sized dust particles, pollens, micro fibers , smog, smoke molecules etc.

Available disinfection methods

Effective? ....Not exactly!

1. Alcohol Based Sanitizers, Soaps, Chemical based Disinfectants: Effective on deactivating viruses, but can’t be used on all surfaces, like chairs, Sofa cushion, equipments which we handle on day to day basis
2. Chemical Fogging: Not convenient nor easy to perform. Leaves chemical residues; spreads them everywhere.
3. UVC Based equipments: Effective at close proximity, due to shadow effect reliability of disinfection reduces, harmful if accidental exposure for eyes
4. Air Purifiers: Effective in Limited Area and on Air born Pathogens, just not effective on contaminated surfaces around us

How it works ?

Vayutron Ionizes the Ambient Air with high Energy ARC generated between high Voltage Electrodes. The ionized gas molecules are highly reactive and are volatile with short life time. These radical Ions when come in contact with water vapors present in air; they form Hydroxyl Ions, convert Oxygen into Ozone and generate a mixture of Positive and Negatively Charged Ions. Such mixtures is generated and exhaled from Vayutron when the disinfection process starts. The Hydroxyl Ions when come in contact with Protein Layers (made up of Hydrocarbons) from the outer shell of Virus or Bacteria, they react and form pores on the outer shell, the virus/bacteria are destroyed and can’t multiply further.

The ions due the excess charge present on them tend to form cluster around suspended air born particles such as odor generating vapors, smoke, allergens etc. Such clusters make these particles heavy and with excess charge, so then tend to settle down on ground, this effectively increases air quality and helps to reduce bad odor. The generated Ions tend to neutralize after the excess charge is dissipated in the environment and come back to original molecular form, so there are no residuals.
